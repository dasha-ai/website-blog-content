## Understanding Voice AI: The Basics of Conversational AI

Before we dive into the AI sales revolution, it's important to grasp the fundamentals of Voice AI. Also known as Conversational AI, Voice AI leverages natural language processing and machine learning to enable human-like interactions between machines and humans. Unlike traditional voice assistants that rely on pre-programmed responses, Voice AI utilizes LLMs, including GPT, to provide dynamic and contextually aware conversations.

Imagine having a conversation with a virtual assistant that not only understands your questions but can also formulate intelligent and relevant responses. Voice AI brings this vision to life, facilitating [seamless and personalized interactions](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions) between businesses and their customers.
