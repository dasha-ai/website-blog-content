## Understanding Voice AI: An Introduction to Intelligent Communication Tools

Before diving into the benefits of Voice AI for sales team coordination, it is important to understand what exactly it entails. Voice AI refers to the use of artificial intelligence technologies to enable [natural language conversations](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/how-voice-ai-can-be-used-to-improve-sales-execution-in-the-e-commerce-industry-with-chatgpt) between humans and machines. These technologies leverage advanced LLMs, such as GPT, to understand and respond to human speech patterns, resulting in highly realistic and engaging conversational experiences.

By harnessing the power of Voice AI, businesses can create virtual agents that can handle various tasks, including customer service inquiries, lead generation, and even sales calls. These AI-powered agents are designed to mimic human conversation, allowing them to engage in meaningful interactions with customers and prospects.
