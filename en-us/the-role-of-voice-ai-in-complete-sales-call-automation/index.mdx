## Understanding Voice AI: The Basics of Artificial Intelligent Agents

Before diving into the role of Voice AI in sales call automation, let's first explore the fundamentals. Voice AI, also known as Artificial Intelligent Agents, utilizes cutting-edge technology to simulate human-like interactions through spoken language. These intelligent agents are powered by Large Language Models (LLMs), such as GPT, and are trained to understand and respond to natural language queries.

Unlike traditional chatbots, Voice AI is designed to replicate human conversation, complete with natural pauses, intonation, and even the ability to understand context. This allows businesses to provide an unparalleled level of personalization and engagement during sales calls, creating a more authentic and seamless experience for their customers.
