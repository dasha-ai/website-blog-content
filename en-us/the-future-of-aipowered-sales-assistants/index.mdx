## Exploring the Evolution of AI in Sales: The Rise of Intelligent Assistants

AI technology has come a long way, and the evolution of AI in sales is evident with the rise of intelligent assistants. These AI-driven assistants are designed to interact with customers in a conversational manner, using [natural language processing capabilities](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/navigating-the-complexities-of-ai-in-sales-a-comprehensive-overview) to understand and respond to their queries. This advancement in AI brings a human touch to the sales process, allowing businesses to better connect with their customers and provide personalized experiences.

One standout AI platform in this space is Dasha. Dasha's AI platform leverages the power of Large Language Models (LLMs), including GPT, to create flawless and low-latency conversational agents for developers. These agents are capable of engaging customers in natural conversations, mimicking human speech patterns, and delivering exceptional customer service.

Another key player in the intelligent assistant arena is Conversica. Conversica's AI-powered assistants are specialized in sales and marketing, helping businesses automate lead engagement and follow-up processes. These assistants can reach out to leads via email or text, qualify them based on their responses, and hand them over to sales representatives when they are ready to make a purchase.

Furthermore, the evolution of AI in sales has also led to the development of [virtual shopping assistants](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/the-future-of-bb-sales-how-conversational-ai-is-changing-the-game). These AI-driven assistants can guide customers through their online shopping experience, providing product recommendations based on their preferences and purchase history. By analyzing vast amounts of data in real-time, these virtual assistants can enhance the overall shopping experience and increase sales conversion rates.
