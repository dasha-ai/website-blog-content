## The Role of AI in Leadership Onboarding

Before we delve into the impact of Dasha Voice AI specifically, it's important to understand the limitations of traditional leadership onboarding methods. The traditional process often involves long, tedious orientation sessions, overwhelming amounts of information, and limited opportunities for interaction.

Leadership onboarding is a critical process that sets the tone for a leader's success within an organization. However, the traditional onboarding process has its drawbacks. In the traditional onboarding process, new leaders are bombarded with extensive manuals and presentations that can be overwhelming and hard to digest. They may struggle to retain the information and face difficulty applying it to their new roles. Moreover, the lack of meaningful interaction can lead to feelings of isolation and inhibit the development of strong working relationships with team members.

### The Traditional Leadership Onboarding Process

Imagine a new leader walking into their first day on the job, excited and eager to make a positive impact. However, instead of being greeted with a warm welcome and a clear roadmap for success, they are handed a thick manual and told to read through it on their own. The manual is filled with jargon and technical terms that are unfamiliar to the new leader, making it difficult to grasp the key concepts and strategies.

As the days go by, the new leader attends orientation sessions that are filled with PowerPoint presentations and lectures. While the information presented is valuable, the sheer volume of it can be overwhelming. The new leader struggles to absorb all the information and may find themselves feeling lost and unsure of how to apply what they have learned.

Furthermore, the lack of meaningful interaction during the onboarding process can be detrimental to a new leader's success. Without the opportunity to engage in real-time conversations and ask questions, the new leader may feel isolated and disconnected from their team. This lack of connection can hinder the development of strong working relationships and impede collaboration.
