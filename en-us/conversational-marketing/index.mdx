## **Main Methods of Conversational Marketing**

So, **what is conversational marketing** really, and what are the main methods of utilizing it? It is inspired by the way that people choose to communicate with one another. When individuals feel like reaching out to a friend or family member with a question or for connection, they will call or message them on a platform or smartphone messaging system. By mimicking this communication tactic, businesses can make relations with customers seem more personal and intimate. 

The main methods for doing so are typically AI receptionists, self-service call centers, and chatbots. There are a few others that may or not be helpful to you depending on your business models, such as AI invoice processors and interactive voice responses (IVR) processors. Dasha AI provides you with the language to implement all of these methods and more. 

There is no one-size-fits-all answer for which method makes the most sense for a business to use. It comes down to individual needs and goals. If conversational marketing is new to an organization, then it makes the most sense to start out by allowing one of the above methods to replace an existing function with the organization. Then, as the organization gets more comfortable with the concept and how it works, it can begin to expand by creating new customer-facing channels with AI technology.

Conversational marketing changes the game by switching it on its head. Now, buyers are the ones telling organizations what they care about.
