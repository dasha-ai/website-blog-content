The Dasha Platform allows the development of human-like conversational AI models. The versatile omnichannel solution means companies can reuse the same dialog across text and voice. Its API-first approach lets companies implement voice AI models within call center environments, as well as integrate them into web, mobile apps and IoT devices via the Dasha SDK. Dasha’s low code/no code Studio GUI lets even a non-developer create human-level conversational apps. Developers and business managers are encouraged to sign up to try the platform out [here](https://dasha.ai/en-us/#RequestAPI).

According to the report, “These vendors each represent five rising trends within the market. As tech allows more sophisticated conversations tied to business processes, application leaders need to stay on top of the trends to succeed.”

“We are thrilled to be recognized by Gartner, which we see as an acknowledgement of the work we’re doing to automate voice conversations on a human-like level in such a way that even non-developers can do it in a few easy steps,” said Vlad Chernyshov, founder and CEO of Dasha AI. “We believe that shaping the future of conversational AI has never been easier, now that we have the Dasha Platform. We invite every user to join us and explore the capabilities of voice AI in automating your business processes.”

Being named a Cool Vendor is as much an honor, as it is a challenge to the company. A challenge to always serve the customers at a level worthy of Gartner’s recognition.

Gartner Cool Vendor reports are released every year. They are dedicated to analyzing innovative vendors and services across many industries.
