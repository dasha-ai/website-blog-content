This technology allows for a more personalized and dynamic customer experience, improving the success rate of cold calls and boosting sales performance.

One advanced Voice AI platform that stands out from the crowd is Dasha AI. Dasha AI utilizes large language models (LLMs), including GPT, to power its AI agents. Furthermore, Voice AI is not only transforming telemarketing and sales but also revolutionizing customer service across various industries. Companies are leveraging Voice AI to provide 24/7 support through virtual assistants that can handle customer queries and resolve issues efficiently. Another key benefit of Voice AI in the realm of sales is its ability to [analyze customer interactions in real-time](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/the-digital-transformation-of-cold-calling-a-look-into-aipowered-bb-sales-tactics).
