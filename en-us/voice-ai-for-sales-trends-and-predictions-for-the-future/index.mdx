## Understanding Voice AI: Defining the Technology and Its Capabilities

Before we dive into the evolving landscape of Voice AI for sales, it's essential to understand what this technology entails and the capabilities it offers. Voice AI, powered by LLMs like GPT, enables machines to comprehend and interpret human speech, just like a real person would. It can understand different accents, dialects, and even context, delivering natural and seamless conversations.

Imagine an AI agent that can effectively engage with customers, understand their needs, and provide relevant and accurate information – all with the same empathy and intuition as a living, breathing salesperson. That's precisely what Voice AI brings to the table.
