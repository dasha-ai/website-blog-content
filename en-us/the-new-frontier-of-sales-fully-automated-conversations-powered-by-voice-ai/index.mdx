## Understanding Voice AI: Transforming Sales and Customer Service

Before we dive into the specifics of Voice AI and its impact on sales, let's take a moment to understand what it is. Voice AI utilizes sophisticated technologies to simulate human-like conversations through spoken language. It harnesses the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT to generate responses that are not only accurate but also contextually relevant.

One of the key advantages of Voice AI is it's ability to [seamlessly replace traditional salespeople](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/ultimate-guide-to-closing-more-sales-in-the-travel-industry-with-dasha-voice-ai) in various interactions. Furthermore, Voice AI is not limited to just sales functions; it has also revolutionized customer service operations. By integrating Voice AI into customer support systems, businesses can provide [round-the-clock assistance](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/how-to-amplify-customer-support-using-dasha-voice-ai) to their customers.
