## Understanding Voice AI: Key Concepts and Metrics in Sales

Before diving into the metrics, let's first understand the fundamental concepts behind Voice AI. At its core, Voice AI utilizes advanced conversational agents, or AI voice agents, to mimic human interactions and engage with customers. These agents, powered by Dasha's flawless and low-latency technology, offer an ultra-realistic experience that seamlessly replaces the need for human employees. By leveraging large language models (LLMs), including GPT, Dasha's AI agents can engage in [natural, human-like conversations](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions), leading to [improved customer satisfaction](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/how-to-amplify-customer-support-using-dasha-voice-ai) and increased sales.

When it comes to measuring the success of Voice AI in sales, there are several key metrics that you can use as a guide. Let's explore these essential metrics in more detail:

### 1. Customer Satisfaction:

Customer satisfaction is a critical metric that directly reflects the success of Voice AI in sales. By analyzing customer feedback and ratings, you can gauge how well the AI agents are meeting customer needs and expectations. Are customers satisfied with the level of service provided by the AI agents? Are they finding value in the conversations? By asking these questions and monitoring customer satisfaction levels, you can gain insights into the overall effectiveness of Voice AI in sales.

### 2. Conversion Rates:

Conversion rates are another vital metric to consider when evaluating the success of Voice AI in sales. By tracking the number of customers who transition from initial contact to making a purchase, you can measure the AI agents' ability to guide customers through the sales funnel. Are the AI agents effectively convincing potential customers to take action? Are they providing the necessary information and addressing customer concerns? Analyzing conversion rates can provide valuable insights into the impact of Voice AI on your sales performance.

### 3. Average Handle Time:

The average handle time measures the duration of each customer interaction. This metric can help you assess the efficiency and effectiveness of Voice AI in sales. By comparing the average handle time of AI agents to that of human employees, you can determine if Voice AI is improving the speed and productivity of your sales process. Are the AI agents able to handle customer queries and provide solutions in a timely manner? Lower average handle times indicate [enhanced efficiency and improved customer experiences](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-secrets-to-successful-sales-with-dasha-virtual-agents).

### 4. Upsell and Cross-sell Opportunities:

Upselling and cross-selling are key strategies for driving additional revenue. Voice AI agents can play a vital role in identifying and capitalizing on these opportunities. By analyzing the number of upsells and cross-sells generated by the AI agents, you can gauge their effectiveness in maximizing sales potential. Are the AI agents able to identify relevant upsell and cross-sell opportunities during customer interactions? Are they successfully persuading customers to consider additional products or services? Tracking these metrics can provide insights into the AI agents' ability to boost sales performance.

### 5. Customer Retention:

Customer retention is a crucial metric that speaks to the long-term success of Voice AI in sales. By evaluating the rate at which customers continue to engage with your company after the initial interaction with AI agents, you can assess the AI agents' impact on customer loyalty. Are customers satisfied and willing to continue their relationship with your company? Are they seeking further assistance or making repeat purchases? Monitoring customer retention rates can help you gauge the effectiveness of Voice AI in building long-term customer relationships.
