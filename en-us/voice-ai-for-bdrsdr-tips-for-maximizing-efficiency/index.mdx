## Understanding Voice AI: Revolutionizing BDR and SDR Processes

Voice AI, powered by Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT, is changing the game for BDRs and SDRs. Instead of spending countless hours on manual tasks, sales reps can now focus on building relationships and closing more deals. Voice AI enables seamless, human-like conversations, providing real-time, low-latency responses to customers. With [tailored conversations](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-strategies-to-enhance-lead-qualification-with-dasha-voice-ai) and natural language understanding, Voice AI agents can engage prospects in dynamic and personalized interactions.

One key advantage of Voice AI in BDR and SDR processes is its ability to analyze and interpret [customer sentiment](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions) during conversations. By leveraging sentiment analysis algorithms, Voice AI can detect subtle cues in tone and language, allowing sales reps to adjust their approach in real-time to better resonate with prospects. This level of emotional intelligence enhances the overall customer experience and increases the likelihood of successful conversions.

Furthermore, Voice AI is not limited to just verbal interactions. It can also integrate with other communication channels such as email and messaging platforms, providing a unified and omnichannel experience for both sales teams and customers. This seamless integration allows for continuity in conversations across different touchpoints, ensuring a cohesive and personalized engagement strategy. As Voice AI continues to evolve, its impact on BDR and SDR processes is poised to revolutionize the way sales teams operate and drive business growth.
