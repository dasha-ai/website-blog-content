## Understanding Voice AI: Defining the Technology Behind Automated Sales

Voice AI, or Voice Artificial Intelligence, represents a groundbreaking advancement in the realm of customer service and sales automation. This cutting-edge technology harnesses the power of LLMs (Large Language Models) to facilitate [seamless and natural conversations](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions) between AI agents and customers. By leveraging sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques, Voice AI enables companies to provide personalized and efficient customer interactions on a scale never seen before.

One of the key components driving the effectiveness of Voice AI is the utilization of popular LLMs like GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer). These advanced language models empower AI agents to engage in dynamic dialogues, understand complex queries, and deliver tailored responses in real-time. Through the integration of LLMs, companies can streamline their sales processes, [enhance customer satisfaction](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/how-dasha-voice-ai-can-revolutionize-marketing-in-the-call-center-industry), and optimize operational efficiency.
