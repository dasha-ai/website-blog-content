Before we delve deeper into the impact of Voice AI on B2B sales, let's take a moment to understand the technology itself. Voice AI refers to the use of artificial intelligence and natural language processing to enable machines to understand, interpret, and respond to human speech. This technology has advanced rapidly in recent years, thanks to the development of powerful LLMs such as Dasha.

With Voice AI, businesses can leverage a range of possibilities to enhance their sales processes. These include:

* Real-Time Sales Support: AI agents can provide sales representatives with real-time information and recommendations during customer interactions, empowering them to make data-driven decisions on the spot.
* 24/7 Availability: Voice AI agents can be available round the clock, ensuring that customers can engage with the sales team at their convenience, regardless of time zone differences.
