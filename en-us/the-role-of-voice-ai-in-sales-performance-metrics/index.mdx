## Evaluating Voice AI: Key Performance Indicators in Sales

When incorporating Voice AI into sales processes, it is essential to evaluate its impact through [key performance indicators (KPIs)](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/navigating-the-complexities-of-ai-in-sales-a-comprehensive-overview). These KPIs can provide valuable insights into the effectiveness and efficiency of AI-driven sales initiatives. Some of the essential KPIs to consider include:

1. **Conversion Rate:** Tracking the percentage of leads converted into paying customers can help determine the impact of Voice AI on sales outcomes.
2. **Response Time:** Monitoring the time taken by AI agents to respond to customer inquiries can highlight the efficiency of the system, ensuring timely and effective sales interactions.
3. **Customer Satisfaction:** Measuring customer satisfaction through surveys and feedback can gauge how well AI agents are meeting customer needs and expectations.
4. **Revenue Generated:** Analyzing the revenue generated from sales activities involving AI agents can quantify the financial impact of Voice AI on the organization.

By closely monitoring these KPIs, businesses can optimize their implementation of Voice AI and uncover opportunities for further improvement.
