## Understanding Voice AI: Definition and Capabilities

At its core, Voice AI refers to the ability of a computer system to understand and respond to human speech. Powered by sophisticated algorithms and LLMs like GPT, Voice AI technology enables machines to process and interpret spoken language, mimicking human conversation. With its advanced capabilities, Voice AI can perform a range of tasks, including voice recognition, transcription, language understanding, and even generating human-like responses.

One of the key strengths of Voice AI is its ability to replace real employees in various [customer-facing roles](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions). By leveraging AI agents, businesses can automate tasks that were once performed by humans, such as answering customer inquiries, providing product information, and even closing sales. This not only saves time and resources but also enables sales teams to focus on high-value activities, ultimately boosting productivity and achieving sales goals faster.
