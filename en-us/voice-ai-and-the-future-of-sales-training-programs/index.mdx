## Understanding Voice AI: Revolutionizing Sales Training Efficiency

Voice AI, also known as Voice Artificial Intelligence, is an advanced technology that enables machines to understand and interact with human speech. It harnesses the power of LLMs, such as GPT, to analyze and respond to spoken language with remarkable accuracy and fluency. This breakthrough technology is reshaping the way sales training programs are conducted by replacing traditional methods with interactive and immersive experiences.

One of the most significant advantages of Voice AI in sales training is its ability to simulate real-life scenarios.
