3. **Design Conversational Flows:** Work closely with your sales and marketing teams to create conversational flows that align with your brand voice and customer expectations. Map out potential interactions to ensure a seamless and engaging experience.

6. **Launch and Monitor:** Once your Voice AI system is up and running, closely monitor its performance and collect feedback from both customers and internal stakeholders. Continuously optimize and refine the system to maximize its impact.
