## Introduction to Automated Voice AI Technology in Sales

Automated voice AI technology is an innovative solution that enables sales teams to streamline their follow-up processes by leveraging artificial intelligence. With the power of natural language understanding and generation, coupled with advanced machine learning algorithms, voice AI can effectively interact with customers, just like real employees. Unlike traditional sales follow-up tools, voice AI has the ability to engage in meaningful conversations, overcome objections, and provide accurate and relevant information to potential buyers, all while delivering a seamless and personalized customer experience.

One of the key players in the voice AI space is Dasha AI. Built on top of large language models (LLMs), including GPT, Dasha is an AI-powered platform that offers developers the ability to create ultra-realistic AI agents that can effectively handle customer follow-ups. These AI agents have the capability to understand and respond to a wide range of customer inquiries, making them indispensable assets for sales teams looking to automate their follow-up processes.

Another prominent company making waves in the automated voice AI technology sector is Cogito. Cogito specializes in [emotional intelligence AI](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/how-to-amplify-customer-support-using-dasha-voice-ai), enhancing the customer experience by [analyzing voice conversations](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions) in real-time to provide agents with insights on customer sentiment and behavior. By utilizing voice AI technology from Cogito, sales teams can adapt their approach based on the emotional cues of the customer, leading to more empathetic and personalized interactions.

Furthermore, VoiceOps is revolutionizing the sales industry with its AI-powered coaching platform. VoiceOps uses voice AI technology to analyze sales calls, providing detailed feedback to sales representatives on their pitch, tone, and key talking points. This real-time coaching helps sales teams improve their communication skills and increase their conversion rates, ultimately driving more revenue for the business.
