## Understanding the Initial Skepticism Around Voice AI in Sales

When a new technology emerges, it is natural for skepticism to arise. Voice AI is no exception. Some sales professionals may question the ability of machines to effectively replace human interaction in sales conversations. However, it is essential to recognize that Voice AI is not meant to replace salespeople, but rather to augment their abilities and [enhance customer interactions](https://dasha.ai/en-us/blog/-ways-dasha-voice-ai-is-transforming-customer-interactions). By freeing sales professionals from repetitive tasks, Voice AI enables them to focus on building relationships and providing personalized solutions to customers.

One of the main concerns surrounding Voice AI is its ability to understand and accurately interpret human speech. However, with advancements in LLMs (Large Language Models) like GPT, Voice AI technology has become incredibly sophisticated, capable of comprehending and responding accurately to conversational prompts. This enhanced ability to understand context and nuances in speech contributes to more seamless and natural interactions with customers.
